WHO WE ARE

15 years of growing green in Hyderabad

Sridevi Nursery began as a small corner garden store in Banjara Hills in 2010. What started with a handful of indoor plants and a lot of love for gardening has grown into Hyderabad's most trusted nursery for home gardeners, landscape architects, and corporate clients alike.

Every plant we sell is nurtured at our own nursery — no middlemen, no cold storage. We believe healthy plants come from healthy roots, and that starts with how we grow them right here in Hyderabad's climate.

Today, we serve 500+ happy customers across Hyderabad, offering 100+ plant varieties, professional landscaping services, and plant gifting solutions for every occasion.
